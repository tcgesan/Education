The interim Government of Bangladesh



The interim government is a temporary government formed to manage a period of transition.The present Interim government in Bangladesh is Dr. Muhammad Yunus . It is formed often following state collapse, revolution, civil war, or some combination thereof.The interim government is formed to run the country in absence of a government.Its main duty is to reform the country from all directions. On the other side, caretaker government is formed to do a free and fair election. It plays a vital role to form a electoral government. It's main duty is to sustain liberal democracy. The interim government is formed on the 8th August 2024 by the decision of the political parties and students.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus took oath on this day. It was formed after three days of dethrone of AL government. Dr. Muhammad Yunus is the chief adviser of the interim government.
